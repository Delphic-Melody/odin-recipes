Cream of Chervil Soup

Description

A refreshing, creamy, reinvigorating meal centered around a mainstay in artisanal French cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large handful fresh chervil
  • 100g butter
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/4 litres chicken broth
  • 3 eggs yolks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 small handful fresh chervil springs

Steps:

  1. Prepare a chicken velouté: Make a roux using the butter and flour. Add chicken broth and salt.
  2. Bring to a boil, then add fresh chervil, chopped with scissors.
  3. Let the velouté, then reheat. Whisk yolks and cream together for a few minutes, then add to the velouté.
  4. Add fresh chervil sprigs, minced with scissors.
  5. Do not let soup come to a boil you fucking donkey.
