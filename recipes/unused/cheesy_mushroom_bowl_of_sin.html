Cheesy Mushroom Bowl of Sin

Description

A delectable mushroom-based treat introduced to us by our beloved husband. A delightful symphony of flavors which uses cremini mushrooms as a base.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds cremini mushrooms dried with a paper towel and thickly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic finely chopped
  • ¼ cup chicken broth or stick or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • kosher salt to taste
  • kosher pepper to taste
  • ½ cup mozzarella shredded or grated
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese shredded or grated
  • 1 teaspoon thyme freshly chopped

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large heavy-bottom skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk the stock and cornstarch.
  3. Add the mushrooms in a single layer and cook undisturbed for about 2-3 minutes, then stir and continue to cook for 1-2 minutes more. You might have to cook them in batches, to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  4. Once all mushrooms are slightly tender and golden, add the garlic and cook until fragrant, for 1 minute.
  5. Add the stock-starch mixture together with the almond milk and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir until sauce begins to thicken, about 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add in the shredded cheeses. Continue to cook until the cheese is fully melted, then stir in the herbs and take off the heat.
